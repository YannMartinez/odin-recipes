Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Vegan Mayo
  • Celery
  • Lemon
  • Red or White Onion
  • Salt, Pepper, any other Spices you want to add

Directions

  1. Pour out chickpeas into a bowl (after washing or cooking as appropriate).
  2. Mash about 3/4 of the chickpeas down using whatever.
  3. Add in chopped/diced onion, celery, and spices.
  4. Squeeze a little bit of lemon over.
  5. Throw in some vegan mayo and begin whipping it all up. Then you should have your chickpea "tuna" salad!
